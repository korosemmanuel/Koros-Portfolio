Project Description:

This is a compact yet functional two-bedroom house design that emphasizes both comfort and practicality. The ground floor plan, drawn to a scale of 1:100, features a well-organized layout consisting of a master bedroom with an en-suite bathroom, a second bedroom, a shared bathroom, a spacious living room, a dining area, and a kitchen. The living and dining spaces are open and connected, promoting ease of movement and a sense of openness. Both a front and rear porch are included, offering convenient access and outdoor relaxation spaces.

The design includes four elevation views, each also at a 1:100 scale, to provide a complete understanding of the building’s external appearance. Front view elevation highlights the house’s façade with large windows, the main entry porch, and the contrast between solid and open elements. Right side elevation shows the side entry and fewer openings to maintain privacy. Rear view elevation reveals the back entrance from the kitchen, designed for utility and ventilation. Left side elevation presents a simple wall surface with minimal openings, contributing to insulation and security.

The roof plan, also drawn at a 1:100 scale, displays a hipped roof design. The slope directions are clearly marked with arrows, ensuring effective rainwater drainage. The symmetrical shape and overhanging eaves offer protection from weather while maintaining the house's modern profile. This overall design delivers a well-balanced residential space that is both livable and architecturally coherent.
