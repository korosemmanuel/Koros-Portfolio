ABOUT ME

I am a GIS Analyst with practical skills in spatial data analysis, remote sensing, land surveying, and GIS application development. I have a strong foundation in both geospatial and programming tools, enabling me to deliver tailor-made, innovative spatial solutions across a range of industries. I've helped with hydrological modeling using the SWAT model, spatial data cleaning and analysis for agricultural information, and setting up role-based access to GIS data. I have experience with software like ArcGIS Pro, QGIS, RStudio, SNAP, Agisoft, AutoCAD, AchiCAD and PostgreSQL.







What I Can Do:

    I specialize in drone image analysis, processing UAV imagery for mapping, classification, and 3D modeling using tools like Pix4D and Agisoft Metashape. My remote sensing experience includes land cover classification, vegetation indices, suitability analysis, and change detection using Google Earth Engine, SNAP, and QGIS. I have conducted spatial analysis and mapping using ArcGIS Pro, QGIS, and RStudio to support planning and resource management decisions. Additionally, I’ve performed hydrological modeling with SWAT to simulate surface and subsurface water flows and evaluate land use impacts.

    On the programming side, I have developed a GIS website using Python, React.js, Node.js, HTML, and CSS, integrated with MongoDB for spatial data access and visualization. I’ve also created a music streaming website (locally hosted) and am currently developing an e-commerce platform with PayPal and M-Pesa integration. I have experience in WebGIS integration through publishing spatial data via GeoServer and PostgreSQL/PostGIS with role-based access. I also implement and manage Spatial Data Infrastructure (SDI), sharing data through public platforms using tools like ngrok. My database management skills include working with PostgreSQL and MySQL for handling both spatial and non-spatial datasets.

I hope to continue my career in remote sensing, GIS, and Data science. My goal is to increase skills in programming and utilize technology to better understand our environment. My background combines GIS and programming, which allows me to create solutions for real-world issues—from environmental studies to online mapping applications.
